About Us

Skaisense AI Labs Pvt Ltd is a pioneering AI-focused company based in India, dedicated to empowering the next generation of talent and delivering cutting-edge AI solutions to businesses. Our mission is twofold: skilling young minds in AI and to provide specialized AI staffing and project solutions to organizations seeking innovative technology interventions.


Founded by visionary leaders with deep industry and technical expertise, Skaisense AI Labs combines world-class education with practical experience.


Dharmendra Prasad (CEO)

Dharmendra Prasad, Chief Executive Officer, is an IIT Delhi graduate with an MBA from XIMB and has extensive project management experience at Tata Steel.

Neeraj Kumar (COO)

Neeraj Kumar, Chief Operating Officer, is an AI/ML specialist and software engineer with hands-on experience in developing intelligent systems and scalable AI solutions.


Our team is further strengthened by seasoned professionals with over 20 years of experience in AI, ML, and data science, blending technical excellence with strategic business insights.


At Skaisense AI Labs, we believe in bridging the gap between AI theory and real-world application. We provide students with immersive, hands-on learning experiences while delivering actionable AI solutions for companies seeking innovation, efficiency, and growth.


Whether it’s transforming young learners into AI-ready professionals or delivering customized AI solutions for businesses, our goal is to make AI accessible, impactful, and sustainable.


Join us on our journey to shape the future of AI in India and beyond.
