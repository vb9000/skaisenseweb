About Us

Skaisense AI Labs Pvt Ltd is a pioneering AI-focused company based in India, dedicated to empowering the next generation of talent and delivering cutting-edge AI solutions to businesses.


Our Mission

To nurture future-ready AI professionals and deliver intelligent, scalable business solutions that create measurable impact.


Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted bridge between academic excellence and industrial innovation in AI — enabling talent to lead, and enterprises to thrive.


Our Team

Dharmendra Prasad

CEO & Co-Founder

A seasoned leader with over two decades at Tata Steel, Dharmendra has served as Chief of Projects, Chief of Project Procurement, and Head of Business Analysis.

His experience in large-scale project delivery and data-driven decision-making now drives Skaisense’s vision of blending real-industry depth with AI innovation and skill development.

An alumnus of IIT Delhi and XIMB, he brings strategic insight and mentorship to build an AI-ready workforce and deliver impactful AI solutions for enterprises.

Neeraj Kumar

COO & Co-Founder

A software engineering and cloud technology specialist, Neeraj excels in machine learning operations (MLOps), CI/CD pipelines, and scalable cloud deployment.

He integrates intelligent systems with modern DevOps practices to ensure seamless delivery and performance.

As COO at Skaisense, he leads the development of AI-driven platforms and skilling technologies — combining full-stack engineering, automation, and applied machine learning to power real-world innovation.

Vijay Bharti

Cognitive Data Scientist

With 25 years in the IT industry, including 12 years at IBM — one of the world’s foremost leaders in AI and cognitive computing — Vijay brings deep expertise in AI, Cognitive Data Science, and Cloud technologies.
